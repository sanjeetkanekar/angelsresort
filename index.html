About Us

Welcome to Angels Resort, Goa

Angels Resort is a beautiful 3-Star hotel in Porvorim, North Goa, India. Situated barely 4 Kms from the capital city of Panaji and 7 Kms from calangute beach, Angels Resort is an ideal destination for visitors travelling to Goa.

Explore home away from home!! There is not much you will miss here. A walk through our landscaped gardens, is like a stroll through the Gardens of Eden. At peace with the world and your own cozy place. Unwind over a Candlelight Dinner at our road side restaurant. A perfect place to try our Goan Delicacies.

Accomodations

Angels Resort boasts of a vibrant cottage styled suites and rooms that are surrounded by well landscaped lawns and and inviting pool for you to relax and unwind.

Angels Resorts has 12 split air-conditioned Suites. comprising of a living cum dining area, minibar,a bedroom with attached toilet and shower. Each room is furnished with double or twin beds, refrigerator, Satellite cable network, telephone and an electric geyser for hot Water to make your room a heaven of Relaxation and comfort.

Our 37 exquisitely designed well appointed split air-conditioned standard rooms have all the amenities that you enjoy in an elegant modern room. All the rooms overlook the refreshing pool allowing you to unwind from the comforts of your balcony.

Angels Resort offers you that personalised touch, so that you have a real pleasurable time with us. Our personnel are rich in experience and offer you the best of their services. If you are not keen in dining out & just want to enjoy your privacy in your own room, just call our room service to serve you in the room.
